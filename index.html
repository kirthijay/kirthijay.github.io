About Me

After more than 5 years of working with friends, I started loving this field of helping women look as good as they feel. “Making people look good also makes them feel good” which in turn, makes me happy. It’s happiness for all...

I am so lucky that I have found a career that I love so much. I have truly enjoyed every minute of my career I wanted to become someone who is capable of helping individuals. Since the start of my career I have volunteered my time and skills at different events. Being a beautician isn’t only about giving facial treatments and selling skin care products; it’s about helping people and making sure they know that they're in good hands.
